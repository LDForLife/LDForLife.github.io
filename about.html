About Sovereign Woman Services, LLC

Our Commitment

Sovereign Woman Services, LLC is a dedicated provider of housing opportunities, with a firm commitment to facilitating access to low-income housing for community residents. We are actively engaged in preserving vital housing units and homes, offering robust solutions to those facing homelessness or significant life disruptions. Our passion fuels our continuous outreach and the provision of necessary resources to aid those in need.

History of Sovereign Woman Services LLC

Sovereign Woman Services LLC was established with a deep-seated commitment to effecting positive change in communities. Recognizing the universal need for safe, affordable housing, our journey began with a clear vision: to create a woman-owned enterprise dedicated to innovating in the affordable housing and real estate sector.

Founder Marie Jean-Louis launched the company in 2022 in Miami, Florida. As a single mother from a low-income background, Marie intimately understood the challenges of securing suitable housing. Her work is rooted in extensive research and education across the complexities of the housing market, zoning regulations, and construction methodologies. This pursuit of knowledge was pivotal in assembling a team of passionate professionals, including architects, designers, and financial experts.

Sustainable and Affordable Approach

Sovereign Woman Services specializes in developing environmentally sustainable and affordable housing solutions. Our approach involves acquiring and revitalizing neglected properties in underserved areas, incorporating energy-efficient features to reduce living costs and environmental impact. This strategy contributes to long-term community revitalization.

Our mission is clear: to meet the pressing demand for secure, comfortable, and affordable housing, offering sustainable, accessible solutions to individuals and families across the region, particularly in Albany and Troy, New York.

Meet the Founder & CEO: Marie A. JeanLouis

Born and raised in Haiti as the sixth of eight siblings, Marie embarked on a life-altering journey to the United States in 2000 at the age of 27, with her 2-year-old son, Louis, by her side. She navigated life in a new country as a single mother, facing rejection from government assistance programs, juggling two jobs, and attending school full-time.

Despite aspiring to business and public speaking, circumstances led her to a nursing career. In 2017, the same year her son enlisted in the Army, Marie achieved a significant milestone by graduating with a Bachelor's degree in Nursing. Her diverse nursing experiences span acute care, long-term care, psychiatric, and correctional travel nursing, equipping her with a broad perspective on healthcare and community service needs.

Today, her journey of perseverance and deep understanding of family struggles drives the company's commitment to making a meaningful impact in the affordable housing market.
